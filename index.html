About us

BLOQspace is a Lisk Delegate formed by a group of ex-athletes, programmers, and crypto enthusiasts with business management experience. With wealth procured prior to cryptocurrency, our main goal is fostering the adaption of blockchain & Lisk through free education, community unity, and philanthropy.

Bringing blockchain and Lisk to the real world is a motivating factor for our movement. That is why we will be opening our first brick & mortar BLOQspace location by March 2018, complete with a Lisk Learning Center! This and future locations of BLOQspace will be hubs for fostering blockchain adaption, free education, community unity, and philanthropy. Speakers will be invited to come and conduct free classes for all ages. In addition, we’ll provide a safe space for p2p trades, an educational mining room, OTC Lisk trading, work stations for guidance on wallets and exchanges, and a Lammassu ATM for education on operating & using Bitcoin ATMs, (we’re working with a developer that’s helping us add LSK to the ATM). This combination of content will be a world-first. We will be using our own stake to sponsor events, create scholarships, give donations, and fund other projects all around the world where we feel Lisk and blockchain can fulfill a need. At least 6 of us are full time working on this project, with many more seeking to do so as we outgrow our first milestones.

Recently, we have been invited to be participants and sponsors of many events, the most recent one being the Bitcoin Super Conference in Dallas, where we had topbilling with some very large projects. We had the opportunity to promote and spread the word of Lisk, and it turned out great! More events are up-coming, and will be announced in the coming weeks, primarily via our Twitter account. A vote for us is a vote for Lisk as a whole. We will be and have been doing provable and immutable works, as we foster a safe meetup culture and a Lisk ecosystem that will be spread far and wide. Sincerely, BLOQspace.io, Lisk Delegate
